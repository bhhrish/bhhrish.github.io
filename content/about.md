I am not a scholar. I am a reader.

Over the past five years I have worked through various texts on Hinduism — philosophy, the Itihasas, and the Puranas. The Ramayana and the Mahabharata have occupied most of that time. These are not simple epics. They are layered, contested, and routinely flattened by the way they are retold and taught.

This blog is my attempt to share what I have understood, challenge what I think is misunderstood, and invite correction where I am wrong. Nothing here is final. Everything is open to debate.

If you disagree with something, I genuinely want to hear it.
